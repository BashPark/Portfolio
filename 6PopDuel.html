Pop Duel


Watch Video

For Global Game Jam Bangalore, I led a team of six to develop a local couch co-op multiplayer game based on the theme "Bubbles." Players control chibi-style characters and battle using different types of bubble gum—normal, freeze, shock, and bazooka gum—each with unique mechanics. As the team lead, I managed development, coordinated tasks, and ensured smooth gameplay implementation.

This project was a great learning experience in teamwork, rapid prototyping, and refining multiplayer mechanics.
